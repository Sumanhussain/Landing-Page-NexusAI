Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NexusAI

What makes NexusAI different from other automation tools?

NexusAI goes beyond simple automation by incorporating machine learning to continuously improve workflows based on your usage patterns. It evolves with your business needs.

How secure is my data with NexusAI?

Security is our top priority. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest, with regular security audits and compliance with SOC 2, GDPR, and other industry standards.

Can I integrate NexusAI with my existing tools?

Absolutely! NexusAI offers pre-built integrations for hundreds of popular business tools plus a powerful API for custom integrations tailored to your needs.

Is there a setup fee or long-term contract?

No setup fees and no long-term contracts. All plans are month-to-month, and you can cancel anytime with no penalties.

What kind of training and support do you offer?

We provide comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, live webinars, and 24/7 support via chat and email (phone support for enterprise customers).

Yes! We offer special pricing for non-profits, educational institutions, and startups. Contact our sales team to learn more.
